Commissions

Thinking About a Custom Art Piece?

I offer a wide variety of options.

Creating a piece of art that reflects your thoughts and ideas can be incredibly rewarding. Whether it’s inspired by cherished memories from a safari in Africa or your favorite animal, I can transform your vision into a beautiful artwork. My goal is to craft something uniquely yours that brings you joy every time you see it, making it a worthwhile investment.

Here’s What I Need from You

  • A high-resolution image to work from.
  • If you don’t have one, I have a collection of images available for selection.
  • Once you’ve chosen your subject, a 40% deposit is required, with the balance payable upon completion.
  • Payments can be made through WorldRemit or Western Union.
  • Depending on the size, the completion time ranges from 2 to 6 weeks.
  • If you’re interested, please send me a message using the contact button.
